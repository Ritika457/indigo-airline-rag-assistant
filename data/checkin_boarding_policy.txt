IndiGo Check-in & Boarding Policy
====================================

WEB CHECK-IN WINDOW
----------------------
- Domestic flights: Web check-in opens 48 hours before departure and closes 60 minutes before departure.
- International flights: Web check-in opens 24 hours before departure and closes 75 minutes before departure.

AIRPORT (COUNTER) CHECK-IN WINDOW
--------------------------------------
- Domestic flights: Airport check-in counters are open until 60 minutes before departure.
- International flights: Airport check-in counters are open until 75 minutes before departure.
- Personalised check-in assistance at airport counters is applicable only for codeshare bookings.

SMART CHECK-IN
------------------
- Smart check-in is a contactless process done via the website or mobile app.
- Passengers receive their boarding pass instantly via WhatsApp or email, without needing to queue at the airport.
- Available up to 60 minutes before domestic departure and 75 minutes before international departure.
- Includes access to features like baggage drop and priority services (where applicable).

CHANGING OR MANAGING A BOOKING
------------------------------------
- Bookings can be managed via the "Manage Booking" section on the website or app using PNR and last name.
- Flight date/time changes are possible; a fare difference may apply if the new fare is higher.
- Changing origin or destination requires cancelling the current booking and creating a new reservation, subject to availability and fees.
- Name corrections: only minor spelling corrections are permitted; full name changes or ticket transfers to another person are not allowed.

BAGGAGE TAGS
--------------
- Passengers can generate their own check-in baggage tags online between 48 hours and 60 minutes before their flight, using the "Recommended Bag Tag" feature.

DISRUPTIONS (CANCELLATIONS / RESCHEDULES BY INDIGO)
---------------------------------------------------------
- If IndiGo cancels or reschedules a booking beyond the defined thresholds (see Cancellation & Refund Policy for "Plan B" details), passengers can rebook or request a refund at no additional cost through Plan B.

CUSTOMER SUPPORT CHANNELS
-----------------------------
- Live chat support available on the website.
- Phone support: 0124-6173838 / 0124-4973838 (India).
- Email support available via the "Contact Us" / feedback section of the website for general inquiries or assistance.
