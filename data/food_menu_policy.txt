IndiGo In-Flight Food & Beverage Policy (6E Eats)
=====================================================

OVERVIEW
----------
IndiGo offers an on-board paid catering service called "6E Eats," available for pre-booking before the flight or purchase on board (subject to availability). Options are grouped into three categories: IndiGo Classics, "6E choice of the day," and "#IndiaByIndiGo" regional favourites.

MENU CATEGORIES AND SAMPLE PRICING
---------------------------------------
- IndiGo Classics:
  - Paneer Tikka Sandwich + beverage: approx INR 400 / $5
  - Chicken Junglee Sandwich + beverage: approx INR 500 / $6
- 6E Choice of the Day:
  - Vegetarian option + beverage: approx INR 400 / $5
  - Non-vegetarian option + beverage: approx INR 500 / $6
- #IndiaByIndiGo (regional favourites):
  - Vegetarian regional dish + beverage: approx INR 400 / $5
- Every pre-booked snack combo includes a beverage; beverage selection is made on board, subject to availability.

AIRCRAFT-SPECIFIC AVAILABILITY
------------------------------------
- Hot meals and hot beverages are NOT available on ATR aircraft (used on select shorter regional routes).
- Hot snacks and hot beverages are only available on flights longer than 60 minutes.
- All food items are served at cabin temperature unless specifically marked "Served Hot."

ALLERGY AND DIETARY NOTES
-----------------------------
- Nuts are served on all IndiGo flights, either as an ingredient or as an accompaniment with drinks.
- Passengers with nut allergies are advised to carry their own meals, since IndiGo cannot guarantee an allergen-free environment on board.
- Passengers with allergies or medical dietary needs should consult their doctor before travel and take necessary precautions.
- IndiGo is not liable for allergic reactions occurring on board.

SERVICE AND AVAILABILITY TERMS
------------------------------------
- If a pre-booked snack item is unavailable on board, IndiGo may offer an alternate item or issue a full refund via voucher, at its discretion.
- Food quality, taste, and ingredients are provided "as is" based on information from the supplier/manufacturer; actual products may differ in appearance from promotional images.
- Prices listed are inclusive of all applicable taxes.
- Food and beverage service is conducted row-wise, following the standard service flow.
- Items marked "for immediate consumption" are intended to be eaten only on the flight on which they are served.

HOW TO ORDER
--------------
- Meals can be pre-booked online at the time of booking or via "Manage Booking" before the flight.
- On-board purchase is also available, subject to stock availability.

Source: IndiGo official website (goindigo.in) - 6E Eats / Food Menu section, accessed 2026.
